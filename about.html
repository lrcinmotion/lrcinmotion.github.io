About Lisa

Lisa Raquel Cowan

Movement practitioner and teacher based in Maleny, Queensland

Based in Maleny QLD, Lisa specialises in somatic body practices, merging different movement modalities and offering short courses in holistic and dynamic movement.

With a B.A. in Dance and extensive teaching experience, she guides individuals to explore their body's innate intelligence to find inner peace through movement.
