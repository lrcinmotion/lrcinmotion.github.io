Personalized Movement Practice

Private tuition offers a deeply personal approach to your movement practice. Whether you're recovering from injury, seeking to deepen your practice, or exploring movement for the first time, Lisa creates a safe and supportive space for your individual journey.

Each session is customized to your needs, incorporating elements from Qigong, somatic practices, and holistic movement to support your body's innate intelligence and promote inner peace.

Format: One-on-one sessions tailored to your goals
Location: Sunshine Coast & Hinterland, QLD or online via video call
Duration: Sessions typically 60-90 minutes
