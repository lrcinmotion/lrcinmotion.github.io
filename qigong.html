Qigong

is an ancient Chinese healthcare system. A mind-body practice that integrates methods of movement, muscle relaxation, meditation, and respiratory exercise to improve physical and emotional health.

Benefits of Qigong ☯️

- Increases vitality and enhances longevity - Balances and strengthens the body - Strengthens the immune system - Regulates the nervous system - Improves bone density - Regulates blood pressure And so many more! Physical –positive influence on heart, digestion, blood circulation, nervous system


Cognitive and emotional- Decreases anxiety, assists with depression, reduces stress
