LRC_in_motion is an initiative that thrives on creating positive impacts, fostering connections and uniting people through the transformative power of movement.

MISSION

LRC_in_motion is about bringing people together through dance and movement practices; physically, emotionally and socially. Drawing on the many facets of dance culture the aim is to build connection, to create, to include and inspire an overall sense of wellbeing and fulfillment.
